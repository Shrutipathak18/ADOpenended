We’re here for those who make the world interesting. Who never stop moving forwards. We continue to search for new ideas and better experiences in everything they do. Because today’s world deserves Web services just as progressive. Those that adapt to your needs, give you control and constantly offer you new opportunities.

EasyChair has all the ingredients you need, from managing program committees to registration to publishing conference proceedings, put together in the same environment and using the same logic. 4,437,464 users and 120,378 conferences made us their choice for conference management.

Our Users

Our conferences range from small workshops with a few participants to very large conferences with nearly 5,000 submissions and thousands of participants. We host top conferences in several areas, for example, the World Wide Web and Bioinformatics.

EasyChair supports a large number of conference models and, judging by our Web traffic, is the world's largest conference management system.

Our Services

  • Conference management , including abstract submission, reviewing, program generation and many other components;
  • Conference registration, with or without online payment;
  • Smart CFP for publishing calls for papers;
  • Smart Slide for publishing conference presentations;
  • Publishing conference proceedings and other volumes;
  • Preprints

We are developing other services, including:

  • Society and professional conference organizer management, where you can combine any of our services to run your society or organization
  • A journal reviewing and publishing platform
  • Membership management
